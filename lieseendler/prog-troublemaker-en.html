HERITAGE & UPDATE_Based on Troublemaker, KNSTFGR_3 represents protest and resistance against restriction. KNSTFGR_3 reveals the relationship between space and protesting body and also represents ways of digital protest. Three performers are spread in the space and form the dynamic with their protesting gestures.

COSTUME_The masekd figure wears a black overall with extremities attached to each other by colored, cut-up pantyhoses. This way the spatial extent of its movements is illustrated. The yellow and pink pantyhoses contrast the black rest of the costume and spot the space colorfully.

COMMANDS
function run_3()
{If the program starts, KNSTFGR_3 runs upright, targeted, in fast speed and attempts to take up as much space as possible;}

function protestSingle()
{If a single performer of KNSTFGR_3 encounters an animated or inanimate object it shows up to six gestures out of the array Protestgestures until the object is out of the way; consequently run _3() is executed;}

function protestDouble()
{If one KNSTFGR_3 encounters another KNSTFGR_3, both stop facing each other and show up to three gestures out of the array Protestgestures in sync; consequently run_3() is executed by both;}

function protestAll()
{If three KNSTFGR_3 performers encounter each other, they all line up next to each other and show all gestures out of the array Protestgestures in sync; consequently run_3() is executed;}
